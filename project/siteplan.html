Portfolio Nexus - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Portfolio Nexus

Reason: The name "Portfolio Nexus" represents a central hub for showcasing personal projects, work experience, and skills in a professional and elegant manner.

Optional Domain Availability: portfolionexus.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a visually appealing and structured way for individuals to present their professional portfolio. It will serve as a personal branding tool, helping users display their projects, resume, and ways to get in touch.

Scenarios

  • How can I showcase my projects in an interactive gallery?
  • Where can potential employers find my resume and contact details?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #222831 (Used for background and main layout elements)
  • Secondary Color: #393e46 (Used for section backgrounds and structure)
  • Accent Color: #ff5722 (Used for headings, highlights, and buttons)
  • Text Color: #eeeeee (Used for general text and readability)

Typography

  • Heading Font: Playfair Display (Used for all headers to give a premium, elegant feel)
  • Body Font: Open Sans (Used for paragraphs and general content for readability)
